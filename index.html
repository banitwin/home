The Challenge & Our Answer

Why traditional methods fall short — and how PINN transforms everything

The Problem

Energy-intensive industries like petrochemicals, refineries, and power plants face massive costs from unplanned outages, rising maintenance expenses, and energy shortages. Traditional time-based maintenance cannot predict failures.

  • Reactive, costly maintenance
  • Siloed operational data
  • No resilience against energy crises

Our Solution

A self-learning digital twin that uses Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINN) to create a live, accurate digital replica of equipment and processes. It doesn't just collect data — it understands the physics behind every operation.

  • Predict failures weeks in advance
  • Optimize energy consumption in real time
  • Simulate crisis scenarios

Four Operational Pillars

What our system actually does

1. Asset Monitoring

Real-time surveillance of rotating equipment, furnaces, compressors, and thermal systems via sensors and SCADA data.

2. Predictive Maintenance

PINN identifies failure patterns and delivers root-cause alerts with time-to-failure estimates — shift from time-based to condition-based maintenance.

3. Energy Optimization

Reduce fuel and electricity consumption by simulating various scenarios and providing optimal setpoints to operators.

4. Crisis Management

Simulate crisis scenarios (power outages, cyber-attacks, natural disasters) and propose contingency strategies.
